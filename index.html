Short Bio

I am a practitioner with experience in diverse projects encompassing software development, information security, agile methodologies, data science, and requirements engineering (RE). My professional interests focus on enhancing software quality through a comprehensive lens that includes proficient use of tools, consideration of human factors, and adherence to best practices within software engineering (SE) and artificial intelligence (AI) engineering.

I received a Bachelor degree in Computer Systems Administration from the National University of Colombia (UNAL). I received my Master in Computer Science from the Pontifical Catholic University of Rio de Janeiro (PUC-Rio) that was awarded as the second best MSc dissertation in Brazil in the area of Software Quality. Recently, I also received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the PUC-Rio. Both my Master's and Ph.D. degrees were completed under the guidance of Prof. Dr. Marcos Kalinowski.

Over the past four years, I worked as a software engineer for AI-based systems at Exacta Initiative. My role includes researching and developing solutions from the requirements and development phase to the testing and monitoring phase. Currently, I am actively engaged in collaborative research that explores the intersection between SE and AI. My research is available in publications.

Outside of work, I find joy in staying active through different forms of exercise. Whether it's running surrounded by nature, trekking up mountains, or playing football with friends.

Feel free to contact me if you are a researcher seeking collaboration or if you believe I can contribute to the objectives of your organization.
