Short Bio

I'm a second-year PhD student in the Department of Informatics at Pontifical Catholic University of Rio de Janeiro (PUC-Rio). PUC-Rio is assessed with a maximum quality grade in research in Brazil and ranked among the top universities in Latin America.

I received a Bachelor degree in Computer Systems Administration from the National University of Colombia (UNAL), also considered as one of the best universities in Latin America. I received a MSc in Computer Science from the PUC-Rio that was awarded as the second best MSc dissertation in Brazil in the area of Software Quality.

I currently collaborate in the Universities R&D projects as a software engineer for Artificial Intelligence-based Systems at Exacta Initiative. My role includes researching and developing solutions from the requirement and developing phase to the testing and acceptance phase. I also do research in the Software Engineering Laboratory of PUC-Rio since 2017 under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Marcos Kalinowski.

My research and profissional experience focuses on requirements engineering, agile methods, software security and machine learning. My publications are available in publications.

Feel free to contact me. Specially if you are a researcher searching for collaboration.
